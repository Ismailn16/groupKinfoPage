Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Password and Security

Reset my Password

  1. Type 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/' into a search engine of your choice
  2. Once the page has loaded up, you should be shown this screen.
  3. You should then be taken to another page where you are asked to pick an account. if you have previously signed into your MyUWE account on the device you are using, your UWE email should be there, if not you will be prompted to enter your UWE email.
  4. You will then be asked to enter your password, and below the entry box, there will be a hyperlink that you need to click that will say 'Forgotten my password' or 'Can't access my account'
  5. If you clicked 'Can't access my account', you will be directed to a page where you pick the type of account you wish to access, in which you will pick 'Work' and then you will be taken to a second page where you will be asked to enter your UWE email and to complete a word verification. If you clicked 'Forgotten my password', you will automcatically be taken to the second page
  6. After this, you are given three options to verify yourself once again, pick the one that is most appropriate for you and follow the steps.
  7. Once verified, you will be asked to enter a new password and to repeat that new password, then submit!

Change my password

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and in the right corner there should be a cog. Click it and a small dropdown menu will appear, click on account and password.
  3. A new page will appear and on there will be your microsoft account dashboard. In the centre of the page is a area labelled 'Password' and below it is a hyperlink which says 'Change Password', click this.
  4. You will then be asked to submit your current password, your new password, and to repeat your new password. Upon submission, your new password will have been set.
  5. In the case that you have forgotten your current password, refer to our 'Reset my password' section.

Reset MFA Authentication

    MFA issues typically happen when you buy a new phone or lose one. In any case, the following steps will help you resolve your issue

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and in the right corner there should be a cog. Click it and a small dropdown menu will appear, click on account and password.
  3. A new page will appear and on there will be your microsoft account dashboard. Among the sections in the page, there is one labelled 'Security info' and below it is a hyperlink which says 'Update Info', click this.
  4. A page with a summary of your security info will appear. In this page, there should be three sign-in methods, phone number, password and MFA. On the same line as MFA will be a hyperlink which says 'Delete', click this
  5. Doing this should delete your previous MFA method and once returned to the security info page, you will see that you can now add a new MFA Method.
