Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Personal Information

How can I edit my personal information

    If you wish to edit your email address, phone number or any other personal information, follow the steps below:

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page is a sidebar. Click the 'Your Info'.
  3. Once you have clicked this, many small sections will show but our focus is 'Your Details'. Under personal details, click 'Edit your details' and you can then edit whatever you wish.
  4. Under personal details, click 'Edit your details' and a popup will appear where you can then make your changes

How to add my address

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page is a sidebar. Click the 'Your Info'.
  3. Once you have clicked this, many small sections will show but our focus is 'Your Details'. Click 'Addresses' and then click 'Add new address'
  4. This pop-up will now appear and you can fill it to add your new address
