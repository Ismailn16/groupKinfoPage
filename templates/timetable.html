Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Timetable

How to access my timetable

  1. Type 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/' into a search engine of your choice
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page is a sidebar. Click the 'Timetable'.
  3. You will then be taken to your timetable page where it shows your lessons for the day, and a chart with your lessons on for the week. This can be adjusted for the month or just the day.

How do I add my timetable to my phone calendar

IOS:

  1. Type 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/' into a search engine of your choice
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page is a sidebar. Click the 'Timetable'.
  3. You will then be taken to your timetable page where it shows your lessons for the day, and a chart with your lessons on for the week. Along the top is a option that says iCalendar, click this.
  4. The iCalendar section will now load and you will be given the option of picking between a Teaching, Exam or combined timetable. Click the one that is most appropriate for your use.
  5. you will then be taken to this screen where you confirm the calendar subscription and upon clicking the 'Subscribe' button, the timetable will be applied!

Android:

The instructions to add your timetable to your calendar on android is very similar to the IOS version. Follow the first 4 IOS steps and then click the link below on further information on how to add it. Be advised, the example in the link is it is an email invite but it is relatively the same if you follow the steps.
