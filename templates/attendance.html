Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Attendance

How to see my attendance

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and at the top is a link to 'MyEngagement', click this.
  3. Once you have clicked this, You are taken to the MyEngagement page and there is a bar with options. Click the 'Attendance' option
  4. Your attendance is then showed. At the top is a summary and further down is the raw data.

How to record my attendance

    Computer/Laptop:

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and at the top is a link to 'MyAttendance', click this.
  3. You should then be taken to MyAttendance and on the main page are 4 sections, click 'Check-In'.
  4. As long as the lesson is in progress, you can then submit your attendance code and your attendance will be recorded.

    Phone/Tablet:

  1. Download the UWE Bristol MyAttendance App from the App Store or Google Play Store. The image below is for the App Store.
  2. Once the app has installed, Log in using your UWE details
  3. You should then be taken to the MyAttendance home and on the main page are 4 sections, press 'Check-In'.
  4. As long as the lesson is in progress, you can then submit your attendance code and your attendance will be recorded.

How to check my engagement

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and at the top is a link to 'MyEngagement', click this.
  3. You should then be taken to MyEngagement. Scroll down and you will see more information.
