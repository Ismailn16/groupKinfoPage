Welcome to Group K's ITS info page!

At UWE, we understand that navigating IT issues can be challenging. That's why we're here to support you every step of the way. Whether you're facing technical difficulties or simply need guidance, our goal is to provide you with the resources and assistance you need to succeed.

Email Support:

Our email support system is your first point of contact for reporting IT issues. Simply fill out our form detailing your problem, and our team will promptly respond with solutions or relevant information to help resolve your issue.

FAQ Section:

Our FAQ section is designed to address common IT questions and concerns. Here, you'll find a comprehensive list of frequently asked questions along with their solutions. Whether you're troubleshooting software problems or setting up your device, our FAQ section has you covered.

Q&A Forum:

For more specific or complex issues, our Q&A forum allows you to interact with fellow students and IT experts. Simply sign in to post your question, and our community will provide answers and insights to help you overcome any challenges you may encounter.
