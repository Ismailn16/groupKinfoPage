Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Finance

How can I review my fees and payments

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page, there is a sidebar. Click the option that says 'Your Info'
  3. You will now see a page with a section in the centre labelled 'Fees and Payments'. In this section you will be able to scroll down to see your due payments whether that be accomodation, tuition, etc. You can also see what you owe to your sponsor if that applies to you and also ways to pay what you owe.

My student loan isnâ€™t appearing on my fees and payments page, what should I do?

  1. First you should confirm that your student loan has been approved and confirmed by Student Finance England. Usually, they email you and send a physical letter to confirm this. If you haven't recieved this, check if your application was successful in your SFE account menu and if it has been then it should only be a matter of time before you recieve confirmation. If it hasn't, SFE will provide information on why.
  2. Now if you've recieved confirmation from SFE and it hasn't showed up, it may be because they haven't sent it through yet. They usually do this all at one go and there is a specific date for this.
  3. For tuition loans, the funds are sent directly to UWE so if it hasn't appeared yet, wait 2 weeks as there may be a delay and if at this point it still hasnt appeared, then contact SFE.
  4. For maintenance loans, the funds are sent directly to the bank account you provided when applying. SFE usually send you a text message saying when the funds should arrive by. They also mention that if it hasn't arrived by this time, to contact your bank before contacting SFE.
