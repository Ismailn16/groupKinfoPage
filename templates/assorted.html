How to create a teams meeting

  1. Download Microsoft Teams or go on the web version
  2. If you are on a UWE account your access may be limited, but in normal instances, click calendar once it has loaded up.
  3. The calendar page should show your schedule for the next week and in the top right corner is 'New Meeting', click this.
  4. You will then be prompted to enter the details regarding your meeting, e.g. Meeting name, recipients, time and date, etc. Click save in the top right corner and your meeting is set and invitations will have been sent out.

How to access Library Search

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and at the top is a link to 'Library Search', click this.
  3. You should then be taken to Library Search where you can search for anything, set filters on your search, explore databases, etc.

How to order a new ID card

  1. Log in to my uwe via https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk
  2. On the myUwe dashboard, on the left is a side bar, click the ‘Your Info’ Option
  3. Once the ‘Your Info’ page has loaded, scroll down and in the centre of the page will be a section labelled ‘Your Documents’
  4. On ‘Your Documents’, there are two sections, and our focus is on ‘Student Card’. Click on the hyperlink at the end of the second paragraph to access the online store where you can order your card.
  5. Once the online store webpage has loaded scroll until your find the Replacement Student ID Card option

How to print things

    This guide is for UWE computers as only they are linked to the printers

  1. Assuming you have a file saved on your computer that needs to be printed. Open this file and along the top of the page should be a 'Print' option. If not there should be a 'File' option, click this and 'Print' should appear when the page transitions.
  2. On the print page, select the settings of your choice, i.e. page colour, page size, double-sided or not, orientation etc. Once you are satisfied with the settings, click print.
  3. The next step is to locate a printer. There is a printer hub on 2nd floor of D block, a Print & Copy room on 3rd floor and a few printers on 4th floor.
  4. Once you locate a working printer, log in using your student ID or your login details and select 'Print Documents'
  5. Once you get onto the 'Print Document', you can pick what specific documents you want to print or all of them.
