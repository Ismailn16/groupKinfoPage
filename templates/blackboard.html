Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

Blackboard

Where can I find my modules

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and along the top should be a option that says 'Blackboard', click this.
  3. Once you have clicked this, a new tab will open and this page is your Blackboard home screen. On the sidebar on the left is an option for 'Modules', click this.
  4. A page with all your modules will appear. Typically, the order of the modules will be in chronological order so if you are in second year, your first year modules will be archived and be at the top of the page, whereas your current ones will be further down.
  5. Once you have found the module/modules you are after, click the hyperlink of the title of the module that is in bold. this will take you to your module hub.

How to access my marks

    These steps will show you your marks in a general manner:

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and on the left side of the page, there is a sidebar. Click the option that says 'Your Marks
  3. The page will then update and show you two sections, 'Marks' and 'Awards and Results', our focus is the Marks section
  4. In the Marks section, there are three tabs, however we only need to focus on the 'Unconfirmed' and 'Confirmed' tabs.
  5. The unconfirmed section will typically show you your current modules that haven't been finalised, and the confirmed section will have your marks for the previous years and the current year if the modules are complete.

    For a indepth look at your marks for the current year, follow the steps below:

  1. Go back to the MyUWE dashboard and in the header should be option for Blackboard, click this.
  2. You will now be taken to your Blackboard. On the side bar, there should be a hyperlink for 'Marks', click this.
  3. You will then be shown a list of your modules. At the top of your page, there will most likely be your archived modules, so if you wish to see your current modules, continue to scroll down.
  4. Once you find the module you are after, the most recent mark you have recieved should be below the module name. If there isnt or if you will to see other results, click the 'View all work' hyperlink.
  5. Now you will be able to see all the assignments you've submitted and the marks for them if they have been given.

How to access lecture material?

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and along the top should be a option that says 'Blackboard', click this.
  3. Once you have clicked this, a new tab will open and this page is your Blackboard home screen. On the sidebar on the left is an option for 'Modules', click this.
  4. A page with all your modules will appear. Typically, the order of the modules will be in chronological order so if you are in second year, your first year modules will be archived and be at the top of the page, whereas your current ones will be further down.
  5. Once you have found the module/modules you are after, click the hyperlink of the title of the module that is in bold. this will take you to your module hub.
  6. The updated page is your module hub and on the left side of the page is a sidebar with multiple options. Click 'Learning Materials'.
  7. The page will update and you will be shown one of two pages. One will have different options for folders for each semester and the other will have options for both lectures and practicals. Whatever page you have, pick the option that you are after. the page will update and the opposite page will be shown to you. e.g. if your first page was for semesters, the updates page will be for lectures and practicals, and vice versa.
  8. Once you have clicked your option, you will be shown all the weeks of that semester up until the present week or the week in advance. When you click the week that you are after you wil be given the option to access the slides and recordings.

How can i access information on my assignments?

  1. Log into MyUWE via 'https://newmy.uwe.ac.uk/'
  2. You should then be taken to your MyUWE Dashboard and along the top should be a option that says 'Blackboard', click this.
  3. Once you have clicked this, a new tab will open and this page is your Blackboard home screen. On the sidebar on the left is an option for 'Modules', click this.
  4. A page with all your modules will appear. Typically, the order of the modules will be in chronological order so if you are in second year, your first year modules will be archived and be at the top of the page, whereas your current ones will be further down.
  5. Once you have found the module/modules you are after, click the hyperlink of the title of the module that is in bold. this will take you to your module hub.
  6. The updated page is your module hub and on the left side of the page is a sidebar with multiple options. Click 'Assignments' This can also be 'Assessment'.
  7. The page will update and you will be shown an array of resources. If you have multiple major assignments, you may have multiple folders for assignments. Although, generally you will find a link to download the assignment specification, general guidance on the assignment, reccomended resources to complete the assignment
  8. Once you have clicked your option, you will be able to access the information, whether that be a word doc, pdf, website, etc.
