Frequently Asked Questions and Solutions

WiFi

How can I connect to UWEs WiFi

IOS:

  1. Find the settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Once settings comes up, WiFi will be towards the centre. Press it.
  3. Once you have clicked on Wifi, there is a section where you can browse a list of nearby routers and hubs.
  4. If you are nearby UWE buildings, 'eduroam' should appear in this list and this is the name of UWEs Wifi, click on this. If you are in open spaces on campus, such as the bus stops or car parks, you may not be within range.
  5. Upon selecting eduroam, you will be prompted to enter your UWE username and password. The username is different to your UWE email, however your password is the same. If you do not know your username, you can find it in the 'My Info' section of the MyUWE dashboard. If you do not know how to access 'My Info', Refer to the 'Access Personal Information' section.
  6. Once you have entered these fields, you should be connected!

Android:

  1. Find the settings app on your android device
  2. In the settings app, Wifi will either be in a section called 'Connections' or WiFi will appear on its own section. If it is the former, click on 'Connections' and then click on 'Wifi' and for the latter, click on 'WiFi' as usual.
  3. Once you have clicked on Wifi, there is a section where you can browse a list of nearby routers and hubs.
  4. If you are nearby UWE buildings, 'eduroam' should appear in this list and this is the name of UWEs Wifi, click on this. If you are in open spaces on campus, such as the bus stops or car parks, you may not be within range.
  5. Upon selecting eduroam, you will be prompted to enter your UWE username and password. The username is different to your UWE email, however your password is the same. If you do not know your username, you can find it in the 'My Info' section of the MyUWE dashboard. If you do not know how to access 'My Info', Refer to the 'Access Personal Information' section.

Windows:

  1. Locate the settings application on your machine.
  2. Settings will open and there will be a 'Network & Internet' option, click this.
  3. Network & Internet will now load and on the left is a sidebar, click the 'Wifi' option
  4. Once you have clicked on Wifi, click on 'Show available networks' and on the bottom right, a pop up should appear where you can browse a list of nearby routers and hubs.
  5. If you are nearby UWE buildings, 'eduroam' should appear in this list and this is the name of UWEs Wifi, click on this. If you are in open spaces on campus, such as the bus stops or car parks, you may not be within range.
  6. Upon selecting eduroam, you will be prompted to enter your UWE username and password. The username is different to your UWE email, however your password is the same. If you do not know your username, you can find it in the 'My Info' section of the MyUWE dashboard. If you do not know how to access 'My Info', Refer to the 'Access Personal Information' section.

Mac OS:

  1. At the top of your page, there should be a wifi icon click this and a small dropdown manu will appear.
  2. In this menu it will have your known networks and another dropdown button that when clicked, shows nearby networks.
  3. If you are nearby UWE buildings, 'eduroam' should appear in this list and this is the name of UWEs Wifi, click on this. If you are in open spaces on campus, such as the bus stops or car parks, you may not be within range.
